KOONTZ FURNITURE SINCE 1919

From showroom to delivery, everyone at Koontz Co. works hard to assure your satisfaction. That philosophy has been at the core of our business, and we believe it’s why our family has been in the furniture business since 1919. We have a large selection of unique furniture and accessories available for immediate delivery.

We will also work with you on special orders to assure your furniture selection is uniquely yours. From working with the architect and builder, to establishing a livable furniture layout and selecting the perfect pieces to work with your existing things, to hanging the final picture, Koontz Co.’s talented staff is with you every step of the way, making the process not only productive, but fun.

    Jill prides herself in being diversified, and she loves the challenge of different ideas. She enjoys design that is a bit unusual.
